About Kaizenify

Kaizen is a Japanese concept that embodies the praxis of continuous improvement. A central concept of Kaizen is that significant results come from many small changes accumulated over time.

Hence for us to become dramatically better over time - we make small improvements, one day at a time.

The surest way we can become better is to become more knowledgeable so that we can gain deeper understanding through applying the knowledge we've acquired. We are therefore able to make better decisions over time.

In the world we live, the Internet is a major tool of knowledge acquisition - for good and for bad. For those of us who choose to leverage the power of the Internet to become better, we are limited only by our imagination.

This is what Kaizenify brings to you... a tool to grow by tapping the knowledge of many others like you, who have or are studying the thoughts of great men and women preserved in books and who use the tools of real time communication and social networks to share and grow.

This is what Kaizenify is about - using the power of the Internet, Real Time Communication via chat and video as well as Social Platforms to aid us in becoming better.
